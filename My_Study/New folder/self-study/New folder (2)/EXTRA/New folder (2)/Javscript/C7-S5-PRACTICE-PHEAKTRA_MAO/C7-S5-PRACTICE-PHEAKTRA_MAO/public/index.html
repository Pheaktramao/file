Our Values

Trust

is unity or agreement of feeling or action, especially among individuals with a common interest;mutual support within a group.

Solidarity

is a relationship in which the holder of property gives it to another person or entity who must keep and use it solely for another's benefit.

Responsibility

is the state or fact of having a duty to deal with something or of having control over someone.

Respect

is a positive feeling or action shown towards someone or something considered important or held in high esteem or regard.

Demand

is a relationship in which the holder of property gives it to another person or entity who must keep and use it solely for another's benefit.
